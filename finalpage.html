Metric: Dimensional Accuracy
Definition: The Assessment Of Deviations In Size And Shape.
Evaluation Process: By Measuring The Dimensions Of The Physical Prototypes. The Measured Dimensions Of The Physical Prototypes Are Compared With The CAD Model To Evaluate The Accuracy.
Examples: CAD Model: 10*10*10 Cm, Physical Prototype: 9.8*9.9*10.1 Cm, Deviation: 0.2*0.1*-0.1 Cm
Reference: Ferreira, J. C., Santos, E., Madureira, H., And Castro, J., 2006, "Integration Of VP/RP/RT/RE/RM For Rapid Product And Process Development," Rapid Prototyp J, 12(1), Pp. 18-25.
